Welcome to Chatro

Your Secure Connection to Friends and Family

Chatro is a safe chatting method with your friends and family. Designed with cutting-edge technology, Chatro offers a delightful and secure way to stay connected through text, files, calls, and more.

Key Features

  • Secure Messaging with integrity verification
  • Effortless File Sharing for all your needs
  • Crystal-Clear Voice Calls with noise reduction
  • Precise Location Sharing with high accuracy
  • Direct Peer-to-Peer Connections

Techniques Used

  • SHA-256 Hashing for tamper-proof messages
  • PeerJS for decentralized, real-time chats
  • WebRTC for seamless voice communication
  • LocalStorage for persistent chat history
  • Responsive Design for all devices
